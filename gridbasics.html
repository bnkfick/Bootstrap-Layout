Bootstrap Grid

Very similar to the original Bootstrap 3 grid. Makes use of flexbox. 12 Columns and x number of Rows.

  • xs
  • col-sm - small screen sizes and upwards
  • col-md - medium screen sizes and upwards
  • col-lg - medium screen sizes and upwards
  • xl

Create a row. Create a column or columns within a row. At what screensize and up do you want to col to occupy x of 12 columns. col-sm-6 means stacked until 576px which is a small screen size. Then each column will occupy 6 of 12 columns.
